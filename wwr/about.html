Our Mission

Our mission is to provide thrilling rafting experiences while ensuring safety and fun for all ages.

History

Founded in 2010, Dominican Rapids Adventures began with a passion for adventure and a love for the Dominican Republic's natural beauty. Our founders, a group of experienced rafters, sought to share the exhilarating experience of rafting on the Yaque del Norte River with the world. Over the years, we've grown into a trusted provider of unforgettable rafting experiences, committed to safety, sustainability, and customer satisfaction.
